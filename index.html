Prism Launcher Installation

Here are the steps to installing with Prism Launcher...

  1. Open Prism Launcher.
  2. Click Add Instance in the top left of the window.
  3. Select an option on the left side of the screen for the pack you will be installing.
  4. Once you've found the pack you are installing select the version in the bottom right then click OK.
  5. You should now be able to play with the pack you've just installed.
